u-blox

product communications

Created and executed security campaign focused on product positioning portfolio. The first phase of campaign focused on u-blox M9 and F9 platforms anti-spoofing anti-jamming and RF interference mitigation. The campaign included publication of whitepaper, series of webinars for sales and customers, blog posts about security, social media posts, paid advertising on LinkedIn and Facebook, presence on tradeshows showcasing anti-spoofing demo and videos published on social media channels.

Security campaign:

  1. Goals:
    • To showcase and promote PS-POS platforms M9, F9 , M10 and F10 as product lines offering reliable solutions with enhanced security features.
    • To educate users about security topics and promote u-blox as a thought leader in the security area.
  1. Target Audience:
    • Phase 1 of the campaign focused on M8 customers mostly companies from tracking and telematics sector
  1. Key Messages:
    • u-blox M9 platform provides spoofing and jamming detection and helps to mitigate RF interference. This means that u-blox chips and modules built on M9 platform are secure and provide the best position even in the most challenging conditions.
